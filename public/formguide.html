How this works — and what's next

Phase 1 (now): read-only. Each task maps to the benchmark judged most predictive (basis: prior); the recommendation is the cheapest of the top-3 — no fabricated thresholds.

Phase 2: sign in with GitHub, report task × model × success (1–5) with optional evidence, vote on reports; thresholds get fitted from outcomes and basis flips to fitted with confidence intervals.

Reports and hypotheses will be open data (ODbL). Full design: spec/FORM-GUIDE.md.
